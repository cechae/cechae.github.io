Dearest Academy

A platform that offers online courses for kids ages 4 to 12

In May 2020, our team launched an education platform for children ages 4 to 12 to access engaging online courses.

This platform allows users to sign up, access educational course contents, and show user progress of each of their enrolled courses on Dashboard page.

Technology used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Webflow for Design
