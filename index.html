About Me

Hi! I'm a 2nd-year master's student studying computer science with a passion for creating user-friendly web experiences. I'm in the final semester for completing my degree at New York University, expected to graduate in December, 2023. In addition to my academic background, I have 3 years of hands-on experience as a full-stack software engineer, specializing in the MERN (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) stack.

Before joining NYU, I earned a Bachelor's degree in cognitive science and biology at University of Pennsylvania. During my undergraduate years, my academic focus centered on understanding the intricacies of computational processes within the human brain. Cognitive science, as an interdisciplinary major, allowed me to combine insights from biology, computer science, and psychology. It provided me with a unique perspective on how the mind processes information and, interestingly, it's a perspective that has proven invaluable in my current career as a full-stack software engineer.

My knowledge of cognitive science has not only enhanced my problem-solving skills but also deepened my appreciation for the complex systems that underlie both human cognition and computer systems. It has allowed me to approach software development with a deeper understanding of user experience and human-computer interaction. My journey from cognitive science to full-stack engineering has enabled me to bridge the gap between the intricacies of the human mind and the digital landscape. It's a unique blend of perspectives that I find both intellectually stimulating and practical in delivering solutions that align seamlessly with the needs of users.
