About Me

Hello! My name is Chungeun. I am a lead front end developer at Dearest, located in New York. I am passionate about web development and modern web design, and creating excellent user experiences. I build websites and web applications with React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. When I work on a project, I always make sure that my project is fully responsive and gets delivered promptly.

During my undergrad years at the University of Pennsylvania, I double-majored in biology and cognitive science. I had a deep interest in learning about holistic approaches to understanding the mind from the perspectives of psychology, computer science and neuroscience. Shortly after graduation, I started to gain valuable experience as a front-end developer beginning in early 2019. Since then, I have always strived to become a better developer everyday by continuing to learn and putting acquired knowledge into practice.

I am also a musician. I have played the flute since I was 9 and continue to have great passion in playing the flute. After graduating from Juilliard Pre-College in 2014, with my continued passion in flute performance, I spent much time in college playing music in the community. I performed with my “Remedy Through Music” college club members as a volunteer musician at a few hospitals in medically-underserved areas. In 2016, I performed as a volunteer musician at the Bundang Seoul National Hospital in South Korea. I enjoy both music and programming :)
