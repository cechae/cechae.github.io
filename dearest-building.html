Dearest Building Platform

Building amenity concierge platform that allows users to sign up, create their profile page and RSVP classes/workshops

Launched in August 2019, our team built a platform for building residents to view all of the building's ongoing workshops/events hosted by Dearest educators.

In the beginning stage of visualizing this project, our team had discussed switching to React Hooks. For the purpose of this platform as a sub-page with limited functionality, we decided to build this page with React Hooks to maxmimize the benefit of simple, consistent patterns to manage state. This was truly a valuable opportunity for me to put my freshly gained knowledge of React Hooks into practice.

Technology used: React Hooks, CSS, and JavaScript
